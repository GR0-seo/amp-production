Scalp MicroPigmentation

Meet patients who chose NHI Medical for SMP

Scalp MicroPigmentation is a medical-grade, permanent cosmetic “tattoo” that mimics the very short hairs of a closely shaved scalp or a short-buzzed haircut under 1mm. It is a new and innovative technique that offers an alternative styling option if you do not want or are not a candidate for hair transplant surgery.
