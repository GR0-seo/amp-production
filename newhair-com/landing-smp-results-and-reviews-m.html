Scalp MicroPigmentation

Pioneered by the founders of NHI Medical, SMP is a non-surgical solution for hair loss. Restore your hairline and regain your confidence.

You Can Look Your Best

Scalp MicroPigmentation is a non-surgical medical grade tattooing process that gives the appearance of hair stubble or thicker hair where thinning is taking place.

It is used to provide the look of a full head of hair for bald men by creating a five o’clock shadow effect. SMP also gives men and women with thinning hair the appearance of fullness.
