Scalp MicroPigmentation (SMP)

Scalp MicroPigmentation (SMP) is a non-surgical way to make you look younger. It will dramatically change the way you look in the most subtle way.

SMP is a cosmetic, medical tattoo that gives the appearance of hair stubble or thicker hair where thinning is taking place. It is used to provide the look of a full head of hair for bald men by creating a five o’clock shadow effect. It also gives men and women with thinning hair the appearance of fullness.

Hairline Lowering

Sometimes you just want to pull your hair back. Maybe you are tired of having such a high forehead that makes you self-conscious about your hairline.

The artistic execution of individually implanting single hair follicles can design and lower your hairline the way you always imagined it. The doctors at NHI Medical will listen to your goals and customize your hairline so you can wear your hair up or down. It’s great to have options and never have to worry about your hairline again.

Explore hairline lowering

Beard Transplants

A patchy beard can be unsightly – it can prevent you from growing out your facial hair. You may not even be able to grow facial hair at all.

With a beard transplant, hair follicles can be taken from a part of the scalp where the hair is thickest, and then carefully placed naturally along the beard line, resulting in a thicker, fuller-looking beard.

explore beard transplants

Eyebrow Transplants

Your eyebrows add dimension to the expressiveness of your face. Over-plucking and other means of shaping and thinning the eyebrows can cause permanent hair loss.

With an eyebrow transplant, this facial expressiveness can be restored. Hair follicles are taken from a part of the scalp where the hair is thickest, and then carefully placed naturally along the eyebrow line.
