    What Can CBD Do for Your Skin?

    You’ve noticed the influx of CBD skincare products on the market in recent years – but what does CBD actually do for your skin? The fact is, CBD from hemp has numerous health benefits, from fighting soreness to effective skin care. That makes it a key ingredient in natural beauty products because the less you do to it, the more it does for you.

    A Key Element in Natural Beauty Products

    When it comes to CBD beauty products, which matters most: the CBD or other ingredients? If you ask us, all of the above. Every ingredient in quality skin care and beauty products matters and each should serve a purpose. Anything extra, including lab-made ingredients and harsh chemicals, will likely just add negative side effects. That’s why we use natural hemp-derived CBD in our products and carefully source other ingredients from nature. But what does CBD itself contribute to skin care?

    Impactful Hydration

    The hemp plant has a lot of beneficial antioxidants, fatty acids, vitamins, and minerals besides just the CBD compound. The fatty acids, like Omega 6 and Omega 3, along with other power-packed ingredients help with collagen production. Plus, the hemp plant provides vitamins B, C, A, and E. These all work together synergistically with our formulation to hydrate dry and tired skin by contributing to the skin’s protective seal while also stimulating skin cells to moderate oil production, whether that means more or less.

    CBD can be efficient at moderating oil production, leading to properly hydrated skin, without affecting your sebaceous glands. Instead, it works with your skin glands to help with peeling or cracking skin, rough patches, and general dryness. Our Night Repair Moisturizer will help hydrate skin while you sleep.

    Get a Youthful Glow Back

    More specifically, CBD extract that is taken from the hemp plant helps maintain healthy, glowing skin. Collagen is key to fighting premature aging and the fatty acids in CBD and hemp seed oil and, when used in our products, help stimulate production of collagen naturally. Using CBD beauty products regularly will help skin look fuller, fresher, and younger. Our Anti-Aging Moisturizer works wonders to restore tired skin and bring back a youthful look and feel.

    Full Spectrum or Isolate: Which is Better?

    You may have seen both of these listed in the ingredients of luxury skin care products. Without understanding the difference, you may end up choosing a product that won’t do everything you’d like it to. Which form of CBD has the greatest positive impact on your skin care routine?

    The Entourage Effect is Real

    At KULCBD, we firmly believe in the Entourage Effect, which means we use full spectrum hemp seed and CBD oils. CBD isolate involves stripping the extract of its other naturally occurring nutrients to only include CBD itself in a product. Alternatively, full spectrum hemp oil and CBD extract leaves those other beneficial fatty acids, antioxidants, and vitamins in the oil. This means greater health benefits and more effective skin care treatment.

    The Entourage Effect refers to how the additional nutrients in full spectrum CBD work hand in hand with CBD’s cannabinoids. When they are all able to work together in a natural skin care product, you’re going to improve the look of dry and dull skin.

    KUL CBD Turns Ingredients into a Luxury Skin Care Team

    We’re confident that once you try one of our products, you’ll see the difference. Whether you want to fight the effects of our environment, aging, or dryness, our products are scientifically crafted and tested to ensure all ingredients work in harmony.
