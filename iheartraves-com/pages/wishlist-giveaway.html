        Wishlist Giveaway

         

         

        Congrats to our November 2019 winner Heather Hunt! Stay tuned for future giveaways!

         

        Step 1: Login or create an account on www.iHeartRaves.com

        If you already have an account all you have to do is log in to that account before you start adding products to your wishlist. If you do not yet have an account on our website, create one by clicking "my account" on your mobile device or "login" on your laptop or desktop browser and then "create an account."


        Step 2: Once you've logged in or finished creating your new account, shop around and add your favorite items to your wishlist. On each product page choose the color and size of the items you love and click "Add to Wishlist" below the "Add to Cart" button. This will automatically add the item to your wishlist. Shop around and add all the items you desire to your wishlist. There is no limit or minimum to the number of items you can add to be entered to win.


        Step 3: Create your dream wishlist by November 25th!  On November 25th we will randomly choose one wishlist created or updated between November 6th 2019 to November 24th 2019 to win all the items on their wishlist for FREE! We will email the winner and announce his or her name on our Twitter (@iHeartRaving) and Instagram (@iHeartRaves). The winner will be emailed at the email address associated with their account and will need to claim their prize by responding to that email.

        We can't wait to give one lucky winner the wishlist of their dreams!

        Contest Rules:

        No purchase necessary. Must be logged into a valid iHeartRaves account and create the wishlist between 12AM PST 11/6/2019 and 12AM PST 11/24/2019. Add a maximum of $5,000 to your wishlist. Wishlists created without being logged into an account cannot be considered. Winner will be chosen and emailed on 11/25/2019 and will have 15 days to claim his or her prize. iHeartRaves will ship the full Wishlist package free of charge via standard shipping once all the desired items and colors are available and in stock. No rainchecks or applying prize to previous purchases. Not valid for cash or cash equivalent.
