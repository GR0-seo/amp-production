        SCHEDULE OF EVENTS

        STORE OPENING
        Wednesday, May 15th 10AM - 1PM

        Come hang with Mama Unicorn, Tiny Dancer, Baby Nova & Pixxie Stixx at our store opening. We'll be there to show you around our store and shop til you drop! Plus, we're handing out 3 FREE SEAMLESS MASKS to the first 100 people to stop by our LUX Rave order pick up center and mention this.


        FREE Kandi Making Party
        Wednesday, May 15th 5PM - 7PM

        Come make and trade kandi with Mama Unicorn, Tiny Dancer, Baby Nova, & Pixxie Stixx! We will have plenty of iHeartRaves kandi to trade and beads and string to make your own. Stop by to do some last minute shopping and spread the PLUR vibes.


        FREE gLIT AF Master Class with @fabulous_Jason
        Thursday, May 16th 4PM - 5PM

        Learn tips and tricks on how to step up your festival looks for a gLIT AF weekend in Vegas! In this master class you will learn everything from building a base to creating a dazzling rave look that anyone can do! Get the inside scoop on how to properly apply @Lunautics so you can be sure your face and body gems stay on all night long! We'll be topping this lesson off with our selfie-ready setting routine.


        Fashion Show
        Thursday, May 16th 5:30PM - 6PM

        Come check cut some of out favorite pieces from the Lux Rave store styled on ravers of all shapes and sizes! Watch these rave babes dance down the runway and get some festival fashion inspo for your weekend in Vegas. All of our models will be sparkling from head to toe with glitter and jewels by iHeartRaves and Lunautics.


        FREE Kandi Making Party
        Thursday, May 16th 6PM - 8PM

        Come make and trade kandi with Mama Unicorn, Tiny Dancer, Baby Nova, & Pixxie Stixx! We will have plenty of iHeartRaves kandi to trade and beads and string to make your own. Stop by to do some last minute shopping and spread the PLUR vibes.


        Thursday Night Lights
        Thursday, May 16th 7PM - 12AM

        Light show artists (glovers, orbiters, poi spinners, hoopers, etc.) from all over the world will be gathering celebrate the beauty of light shows, and create memories and old & new friends! You’ll have plenty of room to flow as we takeover the 30,000 square foot multi-level HyperX ESports arena.


        Get Glittered by Lunautics
        Friday & Saturday, May 17th - 18th 1PM - 6PM

        Sparkle on! The Glitter Gang artists will be offering glitter makeovers small and big. From a simple sweep under the eye to a full face and body with glitter and gems, everyone that attends will walk out of the store shimmering! Fees apply and differ with choice of bedazzlement, starting at $15.


        LUX Looks - Hair Braiding Appointments
        Thursday May 16th - Sunday May 19th 10AM - 6PM

        Get glammed up at the LUX Rave Store with our hair braiding and makeup services by the Luxor Salon team. Hair services include fishtail braids, boxer braids, mohawk, etc and dry styles starting at $20.
