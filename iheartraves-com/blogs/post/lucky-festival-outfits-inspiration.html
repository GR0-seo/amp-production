      Lucky Festival Outfit Inspiration

      Share Twitter Facebook Pinterest google+ Google Plus

      Its almost that time of the year to feel lucky again! USC Events annual St. Patricks Day event - Lucky Festival is returning to the Tacoma Dome on Saturday, March 16th, 2019. The event is stacked with some of the greatest artists from genres of all kinds - its a festival you definitely dont want to miss.

      Not only does Lucky feature an awesome lineup, but themed festivals like Lucky make it much more exciting choosing outfits fit for the event! Out of luck for ideas? Keep reading for some ideas thatll give you rave outfit inspiration for this magical festival.


      Badass Leprechaun

      At an event like Lucky, green is the way to go! What better way than with a matching neon green set?! Pair the set with a fishnet bodysuit (also available in green) and a pair of shoes that will make a statement, like these lace-up thighhighboots.




      Ravin Rainbow Fairy

      This festival look will take you all the way to the end of the rainbow to the pot of gold at Lucky! TheTie Dye Rainbow bodysuit pairs perfectly with theTie Dye light up fur jacket andrainbow thigh highs! Top off the look with some cosmetic glitter (I like this rainbow glitter ) on your face and youll look magical AF.

      Dancing Pot of Gold

      Hit the jackpot with this dazzling rave outfit featuring shimmering gold pieces! TheVelma Fringe Shorts have that glimmery gold look that will make you shine at Lucky Festival. Pair it with theCriss Cross Net Halter Top witheye glitterand youll be the star all night. Of course, whats a St. Pattys themed festival without green? Add inneon green pasties andgreen leg wraps to ensure that you wont get pinched!


      Extraterrestrial Leprechaun

      This festival look is a twist on the traditional leprechaun look, yet still has all the green and gold perfect for this event! Match theglittery alien pasties with thewhite mesh halter top andmetallic green booty shorts. Finish the look withshimmery gold leg wraps for a rave outfit that looks out of this world!

      St. Patrick's Day Accessories

      iHeartRaves also has tons of accessories to spice up your St. Patrick's Day outfit! Check out tons of on-theme items in green or gold colors, such asshoes, glitter, or these adorableFeelin' Lucky Clover Pasties!

      Men'sLooks:

      St. Patrick's Day Themed-Tees:

      iHeartRaves added 6 new St. Patrick's day inspired designs this year, like the Cheers and Chants design featured below! Rock 'em with a pair of gold or green joggers or shorts for the ultimate leprechaunlook. For all the ladies out there that want to rock one of these tees - these shirts also look hot AF with a pair of fishnets and platform boots.

      Ganja Tees/Tanks:

      What else is green and leafy, besides four-leaf clovers? Weed leaves - glorious weed leaves. iHeartRaves has tons of options when it comes toOG clothing. Trust us, you're ganja be happy you went with this look.

      Ganja Galaxy Green Tee

      Green Joggers:

      If you want to go with a more subtle approach to wearing green for Lucky, try a pair of green, galaxy printed joggers and pair it with a black tee. You'll still be dressed to impress (and will avoid getting pinched!)

      Don't miss out on this magical event! Grab your tickets to Lucky Festival.
