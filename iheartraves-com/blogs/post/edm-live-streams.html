        EDM Livestreams to Watch

        Share Twitter Facebook Pinterest google+ Google Plus

        As festival season has come to a halt, many ravers are heartbroken from the cancellations and rescheduling of major festivals and countless artist’s tours, leaving us to rave from the safety of our homes.  

        Fortunately, through these times of isolation, some of our favorite artists have come together to bring us EDM live streams so we can headbang, shuffle, and still rock that bangin' rave outfit, all from the comfort our living rooms.  

        Below is a guide to a few EDM festival live streams, as well as individual artist streams that you can watch on Youtube or Twitch to get your daily source of EDM in until we can return to PLUR-ing in person. 

         

        Don't Miss These EDM Streams:

        Upcoming Festival Live Streams

        Weekly Artist's Live Streams

        Watch Previous Sets Again & Again

        When: Nov 27-28

        Where to watch: digitalmirage.io

        When: Nearly every day!

        Where to watch: https://www.twitch.tv/insomniac

        Dillon Francis

        When: Tuesdays, Wednesdays, Saturdays

        Where to Watch: Dillon Francis' Youtube Channel

        Don't miss your weekly doses of Dillon Francis! These are the perfect sets to take your mind off of things and to get your boogie on! Times may vary week to week, so subscribe to Dillon's Youtube to make sure you don't miss out. 

        Quarantino Khan Live

        When: Every Tuesday @ 9:00 PST

        Where to Watch: twitch.tv/maddecentlive

        Hang with the kween herself every Wednesday! According to her live stream last night, every Wednesday she'll be streaming a mix of live sets, commentary on old sets, q&a's and more!

        Watch Previous Sets Again & Again

        Insomniac Events:

        Relive the magic of hundreds of festivals from Insomniac Events! You can find previous sets from major festivals such as, EDC's, Beyond Wonderland, Countdown, Middlelands and more!

        Where to Watch: Tv.insomniac.com

         

        Minecraft Festival: Disciple Block Party 2

        This bass-heavy lineup took place on a custom Minecraft server and can be found on Youtube and Twitch. Virtual Riot, 12th Planet, and other bass junkie DJs slammed down heavy beats for over six hours each day of the two-day festival while we watched a pixelated rave go on in the Minecraft universe. This virtual rave gave a different outlook on live streaming sets and I would recommend it if you are looking for a different kind of rave scene!

         

        Slander: The Eye Worldwide Livestream 

        Slander’s tour was cut short due to the current pandemic, but the trap and bass duo did not let that stop them from providing a fire and laser filled show for their fans. The full hour and a half set of The Eye tour live from LA was uploaded for all to enjoy and include lots of headbanging, uplifting melodies, live singing, and a brand new Slander collaboration with NGHTMRE.
