        EDC 2020 Outfit Inspiration

        Share Twitter Facebook Pinterest google+ Google Plus

        Ultimate Outfit Inspo

        The most wonderful weekend of the year is just around the corner: EDC Las Vegas! EDCLV is THE destination for ravers across the Globe and we can't wait to see what Insomniac will bring to this year's event!

        EDC Vegas is held over the course of three days at the Las Vegas Motor Speedway and will take place this year on Friday, May 15th - Sunday, May 17th, 2020. According to weather averages, we will likely see temperature averages of 80s during the day and 60s at night. This is excellent news for EDC outfit planning this year. Let us rejoice in the fact that we may actually need a sweater for the evening time! 

        One of the best parts of EDC is planning your EDC outfits!! This can particularly be a fun thing to do amidst the tedious tasks of corralling up your friends and coordinating flights, hotels, shuttles, etc. Wondering what to wear to EDC?  Keep reading to check out the hottest styles and themes that will be a smash hit at EDC 2020:

         

        What to Wear to EDC:

        1. Neon Clothes
        2. Clouds
        3. Trippy Prints
        4. Reflective Clothing
        5. Butterfly Accessories
        6. Rainbow Outfits
        7. Draon and Flames
        8. Mesh Outfits and Layering
        9. Holographic Clothing
        10. Sequins and Sparkles
        11. Fishnet Looks
        12. Platforms
        13. What do Guys Wear to EDC?

        1. Neon Clothes

        Neon has always been huge in the rave fashion game and that makes us oh-so-thankful! Neon rave outfits are such a fun way to express your inner wild-child. From neon bodysuits, to neon rave sets, we've got you covered when it comes to this wonderful trend. 

        Mesh High Waisted Thong, Underboob Crop Top, Electric Neon Ring Choker

           

        Electric Energy Outfit, Step Out Chunky Heel Booties   

        Amethyst Daydream Outfit  

         

        While neon looks great paired with other neons, you can also create a fun, but badass look by adding elements of black.

        Exxxtra Kinetic Outfit 

         

        2. Clouds

        Get lost in the clouds - literally! Cloud prints are such a cute combination of sassy and sweet! Pair it with other cloud prints or with a solid color, like this holographic mini-skirt. 

         Head in the Clouds Front Tie Crop Top, Super Nova Holographic Mini Skirt, Hella Holographic Platform Boots 

         

        Head in the Clouds Mesh Mini Skirt, Head in the Clouds Mesh Long Sleeve Top, Silver Drip Holo Bustier 

         

        3. Trippy Prints

        Make a statement with one of our trippy print looks! These wild and unique patterns are sure to land all eyes on you. Looking for a less bold look? Pair a trippy print with a black item of clothing to create a super trippy effect while still looking cool and sleek.

        Liquid Trip Cropped Cami Top, Moon Glow Neon UV Body Crayon    

         

        Groovy Gal High Waisted Thong, Groovy Gal Mesh Triangle Top, Going in Circles Neon Belt

        Jungle Tripp Cut Out Crop Top, Jungle Tripp High Waisted Bottoms, Holographic Cadet Harness Holster 

         

         

        Thigh High Lace Up Boots, Faux Leather Choker with Mini Ring, Evil Twin spliced Bikini Top, Evil Twin Spliced Mini Skirt  

         

         

         

        4. Reflective Clothing

        Reflective prints are still one of THE hottest looks in festival fashion and we are here for it!! Not only do these styles look amazing, but any photo with a flash will look totally awesome!

         

        Midnight Ignition Outfit  

        Reflect on It Thong Bottom, Ready for Anything Reflective Harness, Reflect on It Wrap Around Top 

         

        5. Butterfly Clothing and Accessories

        Every gal wants to spread her wings and fly! Transform straight back to the 90's with some of these adorable butterfly outfits and accessories.

        Fly Free Butterfly Choker Set, Butterfly Groove Front Tie Crop Top, Butterfly Groove High Waisted Booty Shorts 

        Butterfly Connection Halter Top, Butterfly Connection Cinched Shorts

         

         

        Butterfly Kiss Wrap Around Bikini Top,Butterfly Kiss Marabou Wrap Mini Skirt 

        Baby Blue Butterfly Hair Clips, Rainbow Butterfly Hair Clips  

         

        6. Rainbow Outfits

        Rainbow outfits are a really fun way to express yourself! There are tons of ways to wear rainbow - from traditional rainbow print to rainbow sequins, rainbow checkers, or holographic rainbow patterns, iHeartRaves has endless options when it comes to styling this pattern! You can even create your own, unique rainbow look by mixing and matching different colorful tops, bottoms, and socks on the site! 

         Optic Realm High Waisted Booty Shorts, Optic Realm Cropped Cami Top

           

        8. Dragons and Flames 

        Flames are another big trend this year and we can't get enough of it! Look too hot to handle with these outfits that incorporate elements of flames and dragons. 

        Slick Chich Mesh Underboob Long Sleeve Top, Neva Nude Flash Flame Reflective Pasties, Light My Fire Reflective High Waisted Bottoms 
           
        Please Me Corset Top, Dragon Secrets Cropped Long Sleeve, Dragon Secrets Mini Skirt, Chain of Command Harness 
             

        9. Mesh Outfits and Layering 

        I consider mesh to be the “little black dress” of the rave scene. Every girl simply needs to have a few mesh outfits! Mesh is nice to have, espeically because you can create multiple looks by switching out the tops or bottoms that you wear underneath. 

        Chain Dame Outfit, Demonia Lets Stomp Hiker Boots   

        Holo Star Outfit 


        10. Holographic Clothing

        You ain't no hollaback girl, but you can definitely be a Holographic gal! iHeartRaves offers tons of Holographic items including bodysuits, shorts, tops, and hoods. Just search for "Holographic" on iHeartraves.com for hundreds of options, like these:

         

        Diamond Dazzler Outfit 

        Fluttering Fantasy Outfit, iHR x Demonia Silver Holo Badass Chunky Heel Platforms 

          

        11. Sequins and Sparkles

        Sequins and Sparkles are a must-have at EDC. Frowns will turn upside down once they catch sight of your radiant shine! 

         

        Trip Sitter Outfit 

         

        12. Fishnet Looks

        Whether you wear black, white or rainbow-colored fishnet material as a shirt, crop top, bodysuit or leggings, fishnets will keep you looking hot AF while keeping you cool in the Vegas heat. 

        Fearless Fence Net Bodysuit

        Electric City Spliced Cami, Electric City High Waisted Bottoms

         

         

        12. Platforms

        Give your outfit that little extra 'oomf by adding platform shoes to your look. Platforms come in all kinds of shapes, sizes, and colors. In addition to giving your outfit that little bit of extra flair, chances are that the extra height will be nice for seeing the stages!

        Never worn platforms to a festival? Check out this guide for Wearing Platforms to a Festival

        Demonia Super Stacked Hi Boots   

        Demonia Super Stacked UV Platform Boots 

         

         

        14. Hair Accessories

        When it comes to festival outfits, little details can add a lot! Give yourself some extra pizzaz with some adorable hair accessories. 

        Lunautics Love Face Jewels, Rainbow Butterfly Hair Clips Pack  

        Tinsel Hair Extensions, Lunautics Success Face Jewels 

        Get Bradified Hair Exstention 

         

        13. What do Guys Wear to EDC?

        If you're looking for an outfit for bae, we suggest styling some colorful rave shorts for men with a cool tank top or tee.

        Blue Tides Tank, Blue Tides Men's Shorts

         

        Liquified Tee 

        If you're looking for something to keep warm during those early hours right before sunrise at EDC, iHeartRaves also has tons of cool hoodies for men. Joggers are also a great way to keep those raver vibes and stay warm on chilly festival nights.

        A Night to Remember Joggers

        Be sure to check out the men's section of the site for more inspiration! 

         

         

        If you're still looking for more inspo, check out these pre-styled rave outfit ideas. Never been to EDC? Check out this post for tips for first time EDC attendees.

        Is there something we missed? Tell us what you hope to see in 2021!
