        #iHeartRaveBae Contest

        Share Twitter Facebook Pinterest google+ Google Plus

        It's February and love is in the air! Love in the EDM community is certainly unique and it's definitely something all ravers can relate to. Whether you have a Festie Bestie or a Rave Bae, the best memories at raves are made with the loved ones that shared that experience with you. 

        @cossethmc & @blaabri

        Did you meet your soulmate after getting separated from your squad? Attend a show as your first official "date"? There are so many amazing and powerful stories of love and friendship at raves and we want to hear them! Whether you met your EDM soulmate at a festival or got married at EDC Las Vegas, your story is impactful and part of the greater power of PLUR. 

        In honor of Valentine's Day, iHeartRaves is giving away TWO Valentine's Day swag packs and a guest feature on our Blog! Entering is as easy as 1, 2, 3:

        1. Post a picture of you and your Rave Bae or Festie Bestie
        2. Caption your photo and tell us how you met or a cute story from your time together (the more details, the better!) 
        3. Use the hashtag #iHeartRaveBae
        @blissful.owl & @dir_t_mike

        All profiles must be public to win. Winners will be chosen Monday, February 12th. Good luck, ravers! <3
