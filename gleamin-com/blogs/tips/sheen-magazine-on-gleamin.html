Sheen Magazine on Gleamin

Sheen Magazine on Gleamin


As a woman of color, it can be difficult to find that perfect skin care product that’s all natural and actually works. So, if you’ve been looking for the best all natural skin care products to enhance your natural beauty, this is one that is perfect for you.

“Shining, being the best you can be!”

The term “gleaming” when something is good, looks good or going to happen that is good.

Gleamin is formulated for all skin types but is specifically designed for women of color with deep melanin skin. The hottest trending clay mask is now available to all woman in the United States. If you want to achieve an instant bare-faced glow you should use Gleamin™ Shine Bright Vitamin C Clay Mask. This transformative blend of superfood ingredients works to fade dark spots & hyperpigmentation replacing them with a radiant, confident shine bringing out the best you. The product is formulated from 100% vegan, natural and ethically sourced ingredients including Yellow Clay, Aloe Vera, Tumeric, Desert Lime, Kakadu Plum, Finger Lime Caviar, etc.

Detoxify & Buff

Yellow Australian Clay
Perfect for all skin types including sensitive and acne-prone skin, Gleamin works to revitalize, drawing out pore-clogging oils and impurities. It’s toning and strengthening properties help to stimulate blood circulation, restoring a healthy glow and preventing future breakouts.

Glow & Hydrate

Turmeric + Aloe Vera
Powerful healing duo Aloe Vera and Turmeric, help to protect the skin from free radicals and nasties. Aloe acts as a protective layer on the skin and helps replenish its moisture; while Turmeric has anti-inflammatory properties, reduces dark circles thru stimulating circulation, deeply hydrate and revitalize skin, bringing out its natural glow.

Balance & Heal

The Australian Native Super- Fruit Trio Desert Lime + Kakadu Plum + Lime Caviar
This world-first, powerful and potent blend of the richest known sources of Vitamin C work together to fight acne and prevent future breakouts. Kakadu plum contains more Vitamin C than an orange – 55 times more. That’s pretty amazing. This Super-fruit trio works beneath the surface to heal imperfections and even out skin tone, giving you a healthy, confident glow.

How to use the mask

  1. We suggest applying evenly to a freshly cleansed face using the brightening brush provided in the box.
  2. Allow 10 minutes for the mask to fully dry.
  3. Gently remove your mask with warm water and pat dry using a soft, clean towel.
