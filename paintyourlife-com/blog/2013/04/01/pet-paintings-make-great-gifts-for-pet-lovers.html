Pet Paintings Make Great Gifts for Pet Lovers

By PaintYourLife April 1, 2013 Cat portraits, Dog Portraits 0 Comments

If you’re searching for just the right gift for Mother’s Day, Father’s Day, or a special birthday for that pet lover on your list, consider having a favorite pet photo hand painted onto canvas as a portrait.

PaintYourLife.com has a crew of amazingly talented artists that create brilliant pet portraits, carefully created from your provided photographs on canvas and suitable for framing, for less than the price you might pay for a generic work of art to go on your wall.  These works of art manage to capture the personality of your pet, all the while giving you the look and feel of a professionally painted portrait.

Capture the Essence of Your Pet

Our pets have such short lives, yet while they’re with us they bring us so much joy and comfort.  Anyone would be thrilled to receive a professionally painted portrait of their beloved pet that they can hang on their office wall or above their mantelpiece.  Pet paintings are:

  • Professionally hand painted
  • Created on an authentic canvas
  • Sturdy and long lasting
  • Faithful to the original photo
  • Bright and colorful

PaintYourLife’s professional portrait painters will work with you until you are satisfied with your work of art, and give you the opportunity to comment and make modifications all along the way.  Why waste money on generic artwork for your walls when you can decorate with paintings of your favorite furry friends, hand crafted and suitable for framing?

Attractive Home Decor

Considering all of the money you spend on your pet, from special dog food to medical care, treats, toys and more, why not invest in an heirloom quality work of art that will provide you many happy memories of your best friend long after he or she has passed on?  So much better than a photograph, pet paintings bring your best friend alive, capturing the essence of the animal in a professional canvas portrait.

Whether your pet – or your friend’s pet – is a dog, cat, bird, horse, rabbit or monkey, a portrait painted on canvas is a fantastic way to keep them around forever, and makes an attractive picture to hang on a wall.  For the pet lovers in your life, this is a fabulous gift they will cherish forever – providing it’s a painting of their pet, not yours!  Borrow that favorite photograph and send it in to be made into a fabulous painting, and they’ll be thrilled when they open their gift.
