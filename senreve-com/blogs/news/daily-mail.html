Elegant, versatile and travel-friendly: How Senreve became the latest 'it' bag on the arms of celebrities

Aug 23, 2019
By Annabel Grossman

Priyanka Chopra, Sophia Bush, Anna Kendrick, Olivia Culpo, and Brie Larson are among the stars who have been spotted carrying Senreve bags
The brand is known for creating luxury bags that are feminine but also versatile

Senreve has somehow managed to fly just low enough below the radar that its bags still feel like a well-kept celebrity secret.

But look closely enough and it doesn't take long to realize that these bags are everywhere, nestled in the crooks of influencer's arms, slung over the shoulders of Hollywood stars and strapped around the waists of Instagram fashionistas.

Celebrities including Priyanka Chopra, Sophia Bush, Anna Kendrick, Olivia Culpo, and Brie Larson have been spotted carrying the brand's signature Maestra, while Carly Rae Jepson and Lindsey Vonn own the Coda Belt Bags.

A-list celebrities including Priyanka Chopra (left) and Olivia Culpo (right) have been spotted out and about or posing on Instagram with Senreve bags

The bags are convertible so can be worn multiple ways, whether as a backpack, crossbody, tote or satchel, and transform easily from one style to another.

Senreve's Doctor Bag has also proved to be a celeb favorite, with Jenna Dewan and Emma Roberts sporting the style in Noir, and Julianne Hough seen toting one in Sand.

Selma Blair is such a fan of the brand that she appears to own at least three Senreve bags, including a Doctor Bag in Pebbled Forest and two Coda Belt Bags.

And after seeing Selma rave about her monogrammed Senreve on Instagram, Sarah Michelle Gellar nabbed her own personalized Coda Belt Bag in Noir.

She wrote: '@selmablair I've always said that you are my most stylish friend, and they say that imitation is the most sincere form of flattery!!'


Sophia Bush (left) and Selma Blair (right) are both fans of Senreve Maestra Bags


Kristen Bell posted a photo of herself on Instagram posing playfully with her personalized Senreve Aria Belt Bag in Pebbled Noir

Senreve customers showing off their Maestras on social media and blogs rave about the bag's versatility, noting how well it works both as a work satchel, backpack and as a travel bag.

The bag has proved to be a hit among influencers, thanks to its clean, minimalistic style and the pleasing array of social media-friendly colors it comes in.

Arielle Noa Charnas and Aimee Song are just two of the fashion-forward bloggers who have been seen posing with Senreve Maestra Bags on Instagram, with a quick scroll of the #senreve hashtag revealing hundreds more.


Sarah Michelle Gellar has been spotted out and about carrying the Senreve Maestra (left), and Emma Roberts owns the brand's Doctor Bag (right)


After spotting Selma Blair (left) raving about her Senreve on Instagram, Sarah Michelle Gellar nabbed her own personalized Coda Belt Bag in Noir (right)

But the secret to Senreve's success lies not just in its low-key luxurious, Instagrammable aesthetic, but in its practicality. It's a celeb-approved bag that is not just made for Hollywood stars, but is also adored by female professionals.

The Maestra was born out of a desire to create a luxury bag that works just as well in the boardroom as it does at happy hour or brunch.

Not only does it fit your phone, wallet and lipstick, but there's more than enough room for a laptop, gym kit and a change of shoes.


Arielle Noa Charnas (right) and Aimee Song (left) are just two of the fashion-forward bloggers who have been seen posing with Maestra bags on Instagram

The classic Maestra Bag features a total of eight interior compartments, including a padded sleeve that can fit up to a 13-inch laptop (the central compartment can carry a 15-inch laptop).

Senreve also sells a medium-sized version of the bag - the Midi Maestra - which is slightly more compact than the full-sized version, and the super-cute Mini Maestra which still features numerous compartments and can fit a tablet up to 10 inches in size.

The bags are made from scratch-resistant, water-resistant Italian leather, so you don't need to worry if you get caught in the rain, and they are designed for durability. These handbags are more than just arm-candy, they're designed to actually be used.

Although the Maestra is the bag you're most likely to see on Instagram, Senreve's other styles have notched up their fair share of fans, including the Envelope Clutch (which Saved by the Bell's Tiffani Thiessen owns in Pebbled Marine), the Aria Belt Bag, the Voya Tote and the Bracelet Pouch.

Just like the Maestra, these Senreve bags come in a range of colors, from the classic Noir to Coral, Lilac, Lemon, Blush and Mimosa Blanc.


What's more - like Kristen Bell, Selma Blair and Sarah Michelle Gellar - you can have your bag personalized with hand-painted monogrammed letters in gold or silver.
