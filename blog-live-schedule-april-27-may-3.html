Live Schedule: April 27 – May 3

Apr 24, 2020

Never miss a live row again by adding the Hydrow Live Schedule to your personal calendar by clicking this link. 

Here’s what’s on deck for next week’s live workouts (all times are ET). 

Date Workouts
Monday, April 27 No live rows*
Tuesday, April 28 from Miami Beach 4:00 p.m. — 20 Minute Breathe with Michelle
6:00 p.m. — 20 Minute Sweat with Laine
Wednesday, April 29 from Miami Beach 8:00 a.m. — 20 Minute Drive with Michelle
10:00 a.m. — 20 Minute Drive with Nick
Thursday, April 30 from Boston 10:00 a.m. — 20 Minute Sweat with Mike
11:55 a.m. — Hydrow Duels with Mike vs. James on Facebook Live
Friday, May 1 from Boston 8:00 a.m. — 20 Minute Sweat with James

Saturday, May 2 from Boston 8:00 a.m. — 20 Minute Sweat with Mac
10:00 a.m. — 20 Minute Breathe with James
Sunday, May 3 No live rows*

*It’s time for our Athletes to bid farewell to Miami Beach and return to Boston..! In order to keep our Athletes and crew healthy and safe and to ensure a seamless transition, the team is shipping up North in phases. We will be temporarily adopting a limited Live Row schedule while they make the trip. 

In the event of live row cancellations, we encourage you to check out the on-demand rows, which are being uploaded on a daily basis. See you on the water.

Joining us for live workouts? Make sure to share your pics and tag #HydrowLive on social.
