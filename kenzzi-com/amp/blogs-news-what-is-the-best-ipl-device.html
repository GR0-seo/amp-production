What is IPL? 

If you’re looking at purchasing an at-home IPL device, it is best to get a good understanding of what this state-of-the-art technology is all about. Intense Pulsed Light (IPL) is a procedure that disperses waves of light across a broad area of the skin in order to damage hair follicles and skin cells present underneath. Although the intensity of light is not as strong as a traditional laser treatment, it is effective enough to target the second layer of skin and cause long-lasting damage. IPL is very safe and painless, with a number of FDA approved hand-held devices available for this treatment to be done at the comfort of your own home. 

IPL is commonly used as a method of hair removal, however, there are many other benefits to this treatment which has made it popular in the beauty market. It is also effective in reducing minor impurities on the skin such as discoloration, blemishes, wrinkles, and scars. Regular treatments on the desired areas will work to bring the skin back to its original color and texture by heating and destroying cells of color. With the ability to use this treatment from the comfort of any location, the diversity of IPL in targeting a number of problem areas for women has made the hair removal experience very positive and beneficial for women all over the world.

IPL treatments done with an at-home device require regular and consistent treatments in order to see results. It is recommended to do one session a week, with desired results usually achieved after 12 weeks. While everyone is unique and has different skin, many are able to see a lack of regrowth from just three or four sessions. After the desired results are achieved, it will only need to be touched up every two to three months in order to maintain the results. IPL devices are known for achieving faster results than a salon laser treatment, which are usually seen after four to six treatments spread over six months. Therefore, an IPL device is one of the best methods of long-term hair removal available on the market in providing achievable results in a short period of time.
