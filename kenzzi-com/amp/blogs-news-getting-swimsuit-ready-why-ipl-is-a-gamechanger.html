Bikini Laser Hair Removal: Getting ready for swimsuit season

Let’s be real with each other, it's not a fun process getting the hair downstairs under control. It hurts, it’s uncomfortable and it’s awkward. We completely understand if you dread tending to it every week with the same old temporary solutions. We’ve all been there and done that at some point! 

With swimsuit season just around the corner, you want something that’s going to be stress-free, painless and long-lasting right?

As a problem area for many women, we believe that maintaining hair downstairs shouldn’t be such a negative process. The KENZZI IPL Hair Removal Handet is painless, long-lasting, and very simple – and it’s safe to use on the bikini area! We’re all about helping our ladies out, and the KENZZI is going to make your life that much easier.

Because we're heading into summer, we wanted to let you known why using an IPL treatment is that much better than those traditional hair removal methods when getting ready for swimsuit season.

 

Don’t even think about using that hair removal cream. Hair removal products contain harsh chemicals that are super sensitive to the skin and are not recommended to be used for pubic hair removal. If you are looking for a long-lasting, personal solution to hair removal that you can do in the comfort of your own home, IPL is a great alternative. You don't need to worry about timed treatments and it only takes 10 minutes a day! Did we mention that it's also very painless?

The IPL treatment works to destroy the root of the hair under the skin. As a result, its regrowth becomes lighter and more discolored after each session until it is eventually reduced to nothing at all. By continually using temporary methods such as shaving which removes the hair from above the skin (don’t get us started on the cuts and nicks), its regrowth becomes very blunt and harsh. This can lead to discomfort downstairs when it starts to grow back. Ouch!

After men, ingrown hairs are the bane of women’s existence. They are largely brought on by hair removal methods such as shaving or waxing that use sharp movements. As the hair regrows under the skin causing a small bump, they usually become super itchy and possibly infected if not treated. By using an IPL treatment, the damage to the hair follicle under the skin does not distort its regrowth and therefore cause ingrown hairs. Surely, we’ve convinced you there!
