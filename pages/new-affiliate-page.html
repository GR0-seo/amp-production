Frequently
Asked Questions

What is the T-Shirt Club and how can I promote it?

The T-Shirt Club is a monthly membership where members receive a never-before-seen premium graphic t-shirt for just $14.95. Partners have the opportunity to promote the T-Shirt Club and earn a residual monthly income for every membership they send. Speak to your account manager about this opportunity.

Do I have to sign an exclusive contract?

Nope! We don't require our partners to be exclusive. We believe you should be able to express your passion even if it means supporting other brands.

How is commission tracked?

We use unique links to track all revenue and commission generated by our partners.

How do I get paid?

Your commission is automatically paid on the 10th of every month via PayPal.

How do I get started?
