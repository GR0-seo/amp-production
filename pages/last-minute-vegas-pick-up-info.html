LUX Rave Store Pick Up

Last minute shoppers!

Want to pick up your order in Las Vegas during EDC Week?

Order online today and pick up your order at the Luxor LUX Rave Store in Las Vegas. 

Choose the shipping option Last Minute Las Vegas Pick Up: LUX Rave Store at the Luxor Hotel at checkout.

This shipping option is FREE of charge!

Order online now through 12PM PST May 14th. Pick up begins on May 15th and ends on Sunday 5/19 at 4PM PST.

Pickup must be during LUX Rave In-Store Pick Up hours:

Wednesday 5/15 – 10AM to 12AM

Thursday 5/16 – 10AM to 12AM

Friday 5/17 – 10AM to 7PM

Saturday 5/18 – 10AM to 4PM

Sunday 5/19 – 10AM to 4PM

The LUX Rave Pop-Up Store is located on the Casino level of the Luxor Hotel: 

Next to the walkway to Excalibur
Luxor Las Vegas
3900 Las Vegas Blvd South Las Vegas NV 89119

Please be prepared to show your order confirmation, a valid ID, and the credit card (unless paid with PayPal or gift card) you used to place the order for pick up.

This option is not valid for Made to Order items that require a crafting period. If you place an order that includes one or more of these items, those particular products will be shipped to your shipping address provided at checkout. All items that do not require a crafting period will be available for pick up in Las Vegas.

No refunds or exchanges at the LUX Rave Store.

If you do not pick up your items you will be charged additional re-stocking fees.
