        iHR Promoters FAQ

        Frequently Asked Questions

        How do I earn Commission?

        You will earn 10% cash commission on all sales that anyone makes using your unique referral link to iHeartRaves.com. For example, if your friend buys $100 worth of clothes on iHeartRaves.com after clicking your link, you will make $10 commission. You will not receive a commission on your own personal orders from iHeartRaves.com.

        What happened to the coupon codes?

        Your coupon codes are no longer valid for commission. To make sure you receive your payment on any orders your friends, family, fans, followers, etc make in the future only promote your link. Your code is automatically applied to the cart of anyone who clicks the link. Also, anyone who clicks your link and makes a purchase within 30 days will attribute to your sales.

        Where can I see how much commission I’ve earned?

        You will automatically receive an email notification every time a sale occurs from someone who has clicked your link. That email will show you how much commision was made on the sale. You will also be able to view your accumulated commission on the homepage after you log into your promoter’s account.

        When can I use my commission and how can I redeem it?

        Your commission will automatically be paid out via Paypal by the 10th of each month. You must have a valid Paypal account to be paid.

        I clicked my link and made a purchase on iHeartRaves.com, but I didn’t earn any commission?

        Unfortunately you cannot earn any commission off of your own purchases. Be sure to spread the word about iHR and send your link to your fellow ravers so you can earn cash commission!

        What do I do if I don’t have a Palpal account?

        Paypal is one of the safest and secure ways to transfer money on the internet. It’s absolutely free to sign up. Sign up for an account here.

        My friend clicked my link and made a purchase but I didn’t receive any commission. What’s wrong?

        We use cookies to track sales. It is unlikely but possible your friend has disabled cookies on their browser. If you believed you should have received commission for a specific sale please contact promoters@iheartraves.com and we’ll look into that for you.

        What happens if I don’t promote my link for a while?

        We want to make sure all promoters are actively participating in the program. You have to make at least one sale within a 90 day period to remain active. To access all those awesome perks, your code has to remain active, so don’t forget to spread the word!

        What do the best promoters do?

        Our top promoters post their link on all their own social networks (Facebook, Twitter, Instagram link in bio, Instagram stories, Snapchat,etc.) and work it into their posts whenever they give iHeartRaves a shout out. Message it to your friends on all your favorite chat apps too (Facebook Messenger, iMessage, Whatsapp). The more you share, the more you can earn! Check out our complete promotion guide for tips on how to promote your code effectively! If you have any further questions that haven’t been answered in our FAQs hit us up at promoters@iheartraves.com.

        I'm a coupon site. My code got deleted and doesn't work. What happened?

        We reserve the right to delete codes at any time if we feel they may have been compromised. However, if this was done by accident you can contact our support and we’d be happy to set you up with a new code.

        If you have any further questions that haven’t been answered in our FAQs hit us up at support@iheartraves.com.
