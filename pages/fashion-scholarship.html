        iHeartRaves Scholarship

        iHeartRaves mission is to inspire individual creativity and expression through fashion at festivals and beyond. As a brand, it is important to us to participate in, nurture, and promote the EDM culture that has inspired us. Our community is special, and means something to each and every one of us here on the iHeartRaves team.

        Whether it’s your dream to jumpstart a career in fashion, music, or take the experiences you’ve gained as a part of our rave community to make a bigger impact on other communities in your life, we want to give back to our customers, friends, and followers striving to make a difference. It’s your passion, creativity and self-expression that drives us to contribute to creating a better future not just in our industry but in the world. Our dream is to provide more opportunities for individuals to spread their passion.

        Scholarship Details

        • Value: $1000
        • Separate Scholarships will be awarded for
          Spring 2020 and Fall 2020 terms.
        • To apply, please craft an essay based on the following prompt and email your application to scholarships@iheartraves.com

        Essay Prompt

        Please answer the following in 500 - 1,000 words:
        How has rave fashion, music festivals, and the EDM community positively affected your life and how do you plan to use your education to make a difference?

        Spring Scholarship Details:

        • Entry Opens On January 1st, 2020
        • Entry Deadline Is March 1st, 2020
        • Winner Will Be Announced March 16th, 2020
        • Scholarship Will Be Delivered by March 21st, 2020

        Fall Scholarship Details:

        • Entry Opens On June 1st, 2020
        • Entry Deadline Is September 1st, 2020
        • Winner Will Be Announced September 14th, 2020
        • Scholarship Will Be Delivered by September 21st, 2020

        Eligibility Criteria

        • Applicant must demonstrate passion for fashion and music festivals
        • Funds must be used for full-time enrollment at accredited two or four-year colleges, Universities, vocational or technical schools in the United States.
        • Applicants may apply only once per calendar year.
        • Submissions should be in English
