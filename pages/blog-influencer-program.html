        Do you want to review the hottest items in festival fashion?

        It's no secret that we're passionate about the EDM and rave community. We'd love to hear your thoughts about the ever changing trends and styles of this amazing community!

        Do you want festival outfits and accessories absolutely FREE? We want to get to know you! Please send us an email (kellie@iheartraves.com) and tell us a little about yourself and your blog’s mission. Please include your social media handles and link to your blog.

        We’ll send you a free package of festival gear in exchange for you writing an honest review on your blog about the quality of our product.

        Festival Fashion Blogger Policy

        1. REQUIREMENTS

        You MUST HAVE A BLOG to participate in this program.

        Your blog must either:

        A. Have a following of at least 1,000
        B. OR, you *must have a blog* AND a social media following of over 2,000

        2. YOUR FEEDBACK

        Write a review of your products or include them in a lifestyle article (please include your own photos, if possible)
        Publish the review or article (and photos) to your blog and link back to the product pages or collection on our site.
        You must share your blog post on at least one social media platform (Twitter, Facebook, Instagram (please put link in bio).
        Send us an email with the links to your post after you have completed the tasks.

        3. RESERVED RIGHTS

        We may collect photos of your post and related comments for general purposes. We may use the content in our social media or display on our website. If you do not agree with our decisions, please inform us in advance.

        That’s it! If you would like to join the conversation, we’d like to get to know you a little more. Fill out the form below and we’ll be in touch!
