A Must-Try Holiday Cocktail: Galaxy Bound

No worries if you haven’t picked up a hobby or learned a TikTok dance this year. During this last month of 2020, we have a simple winter cocktail recipe you can learn and will for sure get your holiday spirit up. Our resident Mixologist, Richard Allison of Root Bitters, has created another masterpiece that will be your go-to drink all season. For maximum comfort, this drink is best sipped while wearing our Whirlpool Tie-Dye Hoodie & Joggers.

Now, let the fun beGIN. 

INGREDIENTS

  • 1½ oz gin 
  • 1 egg white 
  • ½ oz lemon juice 
  • ¾ raspberry clove syrup 
  • 2 drops ginger tincture 
  • 4 dashes aromatic bitters

Feeling inspired by our winter drink of the month? Tune in to our Instagram Live on @intotheam this Friday, 12/11 at 5pm PST to see Root Bitters walk through how to put it together and a special giveaway.
