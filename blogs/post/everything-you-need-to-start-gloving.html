      Everything You Need to Start Gloving

      Share:

      If you're looking to start your gloving journey then you are in luck because all of our glove sets come assembled with everything you need to get started. To show you what comes in a complete glove set we will break down our #1 beginner set, the ezLite 2.0 Glove Set.

      1. eLite ezLite 2.0 Glove Set

      This beginner glove light is ultra-bright, affordable, and easy-to-use - Allowing you to focus on the fun of gloving. Each complete glove set comes with 10 glove lights.

       

      2. eLite 4.0 Casings

      A complete glove set includes 10 glove light casings that are used to hold your glove light and batteries together so they fit comfortably inside your gloves. The clear casing allows the glove light to illuminate the whole casing giving you more light to display.

       

      3. Clear Gel Diffusers

      A Diffuser is a small plastic cap that covers the exposed LED bulb of your glove light used to magnify your light trails. With 10 diffusers in a complete glove set your light trails will shine bigger and brighter.

       

      4. Emazing Magic Stretch Gloves

      These gloves are included with every EmazingLights glove set and are intended to hold the glove lights close to your fingers. The white material of the glove reflects more light in order to illuminate your hand allowing others to see your moves more clearly.

       

      5. 1620 Batteries

      Coin cell batteries are used to power all glove lights used in gloving. Each individual glove light takes 2 coin cell batteries to power, so a complete glove set will include 20 batteries to power a full glove set.
