Get to know Dr. Pak.

Dr. Jae Pak graduated from the University of California, Irvine with a Bachelor of Science degree in Aerospace and Mechanical Engineering. He then took a brief detour with the U.S. Marine Corp Officer Candidate School in Quantico, Virginia to pursue his love for aviation. He currently holds an FAA private pilot license. You may see him off the coast of Southern California, looping and spinning in an aerobatic plane.

As an engineer, he designed fiber optic illuminators and lighting systems that are used all over the world. Some of his engineering designs can be seen on the skyline of Las Vegas and stealth ships operated by the U.S. Navy.
