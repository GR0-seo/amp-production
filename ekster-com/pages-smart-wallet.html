Quick Card Access

Ekster® smart wallets allow you to access your cards with the click of a button.

RFID BLOCKING

Our RFID/NFC blocking cardholder protects your cards from wireless skimming.

Trackable

The optional  Ekster® Tracker Card allows you to keep track of your wallet and ring it when lost.

Solar Powered

The removable tracker card runs on solar energy and requires 3 hours of light to last a full month.
