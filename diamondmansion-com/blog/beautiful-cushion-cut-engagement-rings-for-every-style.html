Cushion Cut Diamond Engagement Rings for Every Style

Written by Laryssa Wirstiuk on June 22, 2015 | Updated on June 10, 2016

Share with:


You might be wondering what type of setting will best complement a cushion-cut diamond in your custom diamond engagement ring. Well, you’ll be happy to know that cushion-cut diamonds look great set in a wide variety of setting styles. They’ll look stunning as a solitaire or surrounded by other diamonds. In this post, we show you cushion-cut diamonds paired with vintage, classic, trendy, modern, and celebrity engagement ring styles.

Vintage – Our custom-designed, vintage-inspired cushion cut engagement rings are reminiscent of old-world glamour without looking outdated. These vintage looks have stood the test of time and are simply stunning on the bride who treasures the past. Because we have offer variety of gold colors and setting styles, you’ll be able to give a nod to the past without sacrificing any personal preferences.

1.2 CT Natural Diamond Cushion Cut Verragio 3-Stone Insignia Infinity Pave Designer Engagement Ring Platinum GIA
2.2CT Yellow Diamond Cushion Cut Infinity Pave Insignia Verragio Vintage Natural Diamond Bridal Set Yellow Gold GIA
2.2CT Natural Diamond Cushion Cut Natural Channel pave Diamond Split Shank Engagement Ring 14K Rose Gold GIA

Classic – Our custom-designed, classic, cushion-cut engagement rings possess a simple elegance. Despite their simplicity, they’re anything but boring. These diamonds dazzle on everything from simple, thin bands to elaborate, thick bands. You can’t go wrong with one of these timeless looks.

Trendy – The trendy cushion-cut diamond engagement rings below feature interesting and unusual details. The diamonds look stunning set in their intricate bands. Consider incorporating two different color diamonds for a look that is so “now.” May we also suggest combining two setting styles in a customized design? The result is a truly one-of-a-kind ring that reflects your personal style.

Modern – The details on our modern cushion-cut engagement rings elevate classic styles to reflect the taste of today’s bride. Our modern collection contains a variety of fantastic choices. You can create a modern masterpiece from our selection of settings.

These rings feature stunning stones with beautiful diamond detailing on the bands and around the stone.
