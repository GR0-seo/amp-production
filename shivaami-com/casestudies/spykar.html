Spykar is synonymous with the ‘Young & Restless’ generation of today. Keeping up with the ever-changing dynamics of the global fashion industry, the collection exudes an individualistic and contemporary style.

  • Headquarters : Mumbai
  • Industry : Manufacture
  • Legacy Systems : Ice Warp
  • Number of Employees : 350

IceWarp server was having an issue in downtime, being a manufacture company delay in the mail services was not negotiable to management. It was the pain for IT staff to convince the management on the downtime.

Choosing Google Workspace was the must for these challenges

Shivaami guided Spykar through a comprehensive migration process via IMAP for key users, rest PST migration guidance was given

Shivaami project management team conducted meetings on an execution of the project.

The deployment services consisted of

  • Provisioning Google Workspace Account.
  • Migrating legacy email from their email clients and email server data to Google Workspace.
  • Mail Routing.
  • Device Management.
  • Document Collaboration using Google Docs and Drive.
  • File storage and File share using Google Drive.
  • Change Management and online training.

The process led the business to deploy a range of Google Cloud products and services

An added advantage of adopting Google Workspace has been increased customer interaction. Because so many customers use Gmail

Employees now regularly use Google Hangouts Chat and Hangouts Meet

Google Workspace Reports alerts and reporting helped IT guys to manage the domain security.

Enabling device management policy allowed the internal IT team to control Gmail setup accounts.

The challenges with their expectation were fulfilled with Google Workspace
