Forbes Names Nunbelievable Cookies a Perfect Holiday Gift That Gives Back

  • by Nunbelievable Inc.
  • 02 December, 2020
  • 1 min read

Nunbelievable cookies are the perfect holiday gift, especially if you want to give a present that makes a positive impact in the world. And don’t just take our word for it:  Forbes recently included our delicious handcrafted cookies in their holiday gift guide for gifts that give back.


“These soft-bake cookies may have a funny name but they are seriously good,” Lois Alter Mark wrote in herrecent round-up of gift ideas. “Each cookie comes in its own package and is non-GMO and preservative-free with no artificial flavors. They are the perfect travel snacks, and you’ll want to buy them by the carton or sign up for a monthly subscription.”


The story also highlights the Nunbelievable mission: To make a difference in the fight against hunger. For every cookie you buy, we donate one meal to one of our impact partners. (You can read the full story atforbes.com.)


So there you have it: Our cookies are delicious and they help make a difference in the world. They’re the ideal gift for family, friends, coworkers, clients — or yourself! Order them now at nunbelievable.com or Amazon.
