What equipment do I need for JNL Fusion?

  • Modified on: Tue, Mar, 2020
  • at 9:12 am

JNL Fusion requires the use of small hand weights or lightweight bands. It also uses either cordless jump ropes or a corded jump rope. The jump ropes can be replaced with running in place to raise your heart rate, as well. All items can be purchased through an online retailer like Amazon or at any sporting goods store.
