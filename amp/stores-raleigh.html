Helpful FAQs

  • Can I take the mattress home with me?

    Not a problem! We actually dont carry mattress inventory in our stores, so dont worry about trying to fit a box in your car. Once you try the bed out, wed be happy to help you place your order in the store, or you can order it online from home. It will arrive on your doorstep within a few days via FedEx Ground at no extra cost to you.

  • What forms of payment are accepted?

    We accept payment via Visa, MasterCard, Discover, and American Express. We do not accept cash or checks in our stores.

  • Do you offer financing?

    Yes! We have partnered with Affirm to offer financing or pay-over-time options for our customers. At checkout, you will be given the option to pay through Affirm. If approved, you will be given loan options for 3, 6, or 12 months. All interest is provided up front, there are no hidden fees, and only a soft credit check is done through Affirm's system for approval. After you've confirmed your choice, you will be redirected back to our website to complete checkout. That's all there is to it!
