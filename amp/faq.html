Popular FAQs

  • How do I return my mattress?

    Please reach out to us by emailing support@tn.com to start your return within your 100-Night Trial. From there, we’ll help you to donate it to a local charity or non-profit organization.

    We’ll find a donation location for you, but you’re welcome to donate to any organization you’d like to support. If we’re unable to find a donation location in your area, we'll coordinate with a 3rd party pickup service free of charge.

    After your mattress is donated, you will upload a scanned or photographed copy of your donation receipt using a link we provide. We'll process your refund after receiving the donation receipt. It takes 5-10 business days for your refund to return to your payment card or account.

    Please note Hawaii, Alaska, and same-day shipping fees are non-refundable.

    Please note that if you purchased from Amazon, Lowe's, or Walmart, their return policy will be different. Items purchased through these third-party companies will need to be returned directly to them by their preferred return methods.

    You can learn more about Amazon returns here and Lowe's returns here .

    Our return policy does only allow for one return per household per year, so we can only return and refund one mattress purchased within the same year.

  • What's the difference between ordering through Amazon and Tuft & Needle?

    The return process is the biggest difference when ordering through Amazon. All returns will go directly through Amazon Customer Service. Each product has the same 100-night trial and free returns. The only exceptions are pillows and orders placed through Amazon Canada which have a 30-night sleep trial.

  • How do I unbox my mattress?

    Position your mattress on top of your bed frame and find the ends of the plastic wrap stuck to the side of the roll. Pull on the end of the plastic until the mattress is completely unrolled. Roll the mattress out so that it's flat, then pull off the remaining plastic. The mattress will begin expanding immediately, and you’re done.

    Please note, the mattress will look smaller than normal size until you remove the plastic. If you'd like to see a video of how to unbox your mattress, check it out here .

    For a more detailed overview on what to do when your mattress arrives, check out this article on our blog.

  • How do I return my T&N products if I purchased through Amazon?

    Any T&N products purchased on Amazon will be returned directly through Amazon.

    To start a return with Amazon for your mattress within the 100-Night Sleep Trial, call (866) 423-5353 to reach Amazon’s Special Handling Department. They will be best able to help with your mattress return. Returns through Special Handling do not require you to ship the mattress back to Amazon.

    To return any product within their individual trial period such as a pillow, the Amazon exclusive Nod mattress, protector, pouch, topper, metal base, or box foundation, you will start and complete your return online through your Amazon account.

  • How long will it take for my order to arrive?

    For standard shipping, delivery times will vary. You can usually expect delivery of your ordered items within 3-7 business days by FedEx Ground, with delivery Monday through Friday, excluding holidays.

    To get a shipping estimate prior to purchase, add the items you wish to order to your cart. Follow the checkout flow and after inputting your shipping address, an ETA will show for each item. There is no need to input your payment information to check.

    All items ship separately. You will receive a FedEx Ground tracking email for each item as it ships out. The FedEx tracking information linked in the email will automatically update with the most accurate estimated delivery date as it travels to your home.
