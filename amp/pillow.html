How we built it

Armed with our T&N Adaptive ® Foam, we adjusted the density and firmness to the ideal comfort for a pillow that would pair perfectly with your T&N or Mint Mattress.

  1. Unbelievably soft cover

    We carefully constructed our washable cover to be breathable while remaining incredibly soft and perfectly plush.

  2. Soft yet supportive foam core

    Our pillow is designed to work for most people. It feels plush yet supportive, and every side sleeps cool—no need to keep flipping your pillow.

What we didn't use

Our competitors use a wide range of materials. Here is why we don't.

Down

Down is super soft and a great materials for many things, however down pillows tend to lack support and require constant fluffing.

Fiber

This synthetic down-alternative frequently shifts to wherever your head isn't and flattens or develops hollow spots over time.

Shredded Foam

Shredded foam pillows are usually made from lumpy, leftover foam sold in large quantities to pillow manufacturers. You never know what you’re getting.

Molded Foam

Extremely dense and with a feel that is dependent on the temperature in your room, molded foam pillows also usually have a stiff skin that inhibits breathability.

Ready to order, but have questions?

  • Will my T&N pillow fit in my pillow case?

    Our pillows are Standard size (16"L x 25"W x 5"H) and King size (16"L x 34"W x 5"H), which will fit in any Standard or King size pillow case, respectively.

  • Can I wash my T&N pillow?

    You can take off and wash the unbelievably soft pillow cover and let it air-dry. Do not wash the pillow itself.

  • How will my T&N pillow ship to me?

    Your pillow will be delivered in a small box right to your door via FedEx.

  • What’s this risk-free return policy all about?

    We want you to feel good trying your new pillow out in the comfort of your home. With this in mind, you can rest assured you’ll never end up stuck with a pillow that’s not perfect for you. If your pillow doesn’t work for any reason within your 100-Night Trial, you can return it for a full refund by following these three simple steps:

    1. Email ( support@tuftandneedle.com ) or call ( 888-677-9331 ) into our support team letting them know you’re looking to return the pillow.

    2. We’ll send you a FedEx return label to print out and stick on the box.

    3. Drop that box off at your local FedEx store.

    4. Refunds will automatically be credited back on your card between 3 and 7 days after you drop the package off.

  • What does the 3-Year Limited Warranty cover?

    We stand behind our pillows and offer a 3-year limited warranty to prove it. This warranty is limited to material defects resulting from defective materials or workmanship in the pillow. To read more about our pillow warranty, please click here .
