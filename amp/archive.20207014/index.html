    WHY SHOP AT IHEARTRAVES?

    iHeartRaves' mission is to inspire individual creativity and self-expression through rave clothing and festival fashion at EDM events, music festivals, EDC, and beyond. We strive to bring in the hottest rave wear trends because we know raver clothing should be far from basic and boring. In 2010, we saw that cute, affordable rave apparel from a reliable company was hard to come by and decided that we could make high quality, fashionable rave clothing for women and men more easily accessible. Our journey and growth has led us to become the world’s #1 rave clothing store, all thanks to you!

    RAVE FASHION TRENDSETTERS

    Calling all EDM girls and guys! iHeartRaves has just what you’re looking for festival wear and beyond. From couples rave costumes to the latest rave outfit ideas , shopping for that perfect rave outfit has never been easier. With hundreds of styles of rave tank tops, shirts, hoodies, and shorts, rave clothing for men has never looked this good! Our festival shop can be described as diverse, colorful, and sparkly! We’ve even been referred to as a "rainbow clothing store."

    If bright and shiny, isn’t your look, we have tons of darker, more alternative looks to outfit your mood. Want to be a disco queen? Check out our selection of holographic clothing and disco outfits. Channeling some 80s vibes? We have tons of neon clothes.

    We even have rave gear to outfit your whole squad. With new, exclusive all over print clothing and trippy rave bandana styles coming out regularly, there’s always something new to keep your style looking on point! Everyone on your squad will scream, "I Heart Raves!" Pair our super soft fluffies with our one piece bodysuits or hosiery for a great rave look or try out a halter top with some high waisted shorts for a boho look. Our wildly unique music festival apparel and EDM clothing answers the ultimate question: What to wear to a rave?

    BEYOND THE MUSIC FESTIVAL

    Today, our selection of festival wear became bigger and better than ever. We are now able to offer everything you can imagine for festival clothes and beyond! In 2019, we were able to curate lifestyle collections to provide clothing for EDM girls that want to shop beyond the rave. If you’re looking for something to the rave and beyond, check out our huge selection of boho outfits, 90s themed clothing, or our badass utility wear. Our lifestyle collections offer everything from: denim, rainbow tees, crop tops, floral print and maxi dresses. Wear your outfits to brunch with the gals, or mix n match pieces with rave accessories for a perfect festival wear look.

    In our lifestyle collections, you’ll find everything you need to wear in every day life.

    MUSIC UNITES US ALL

    Each member of the iHeartRaves team shares a love of dance music and lives for late night festival adventures (and of course, rave kandi!). Our passion for dance music and rave apparel inspires us to design, create, and supply the most innovative products, including music festival clothing and beyond for ravers across the globe. Whether you’re looking for EDC outfits , club outfits, club dresses or other music festival outfits, iHeartRaves is your one stop festival shop! We truly value the culture of the EDM community and are dedicated to the growth. This is a community we hold close to our hearts. It’s very important to us that we stay heavily involved in the EDM scene so that we are able to thoughtfully curate our rave and boho clothing collections to provide ravers with a way to showcase their personal style through rave clothes and EDM outfits.
