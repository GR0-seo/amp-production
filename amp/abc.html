Our customer feedback is the answer to your question

How can you trust reviews from the people who stand to profit most?

When it comes to reviews, we like to leave traditional reviews to trusted brands like Google and Amazon, so you can get unbiased information without worrying about where the reviews came from. We know we’re biased and so do you.

130,000+ Reviews

However, in our continued value of transparency, we wanted to make our customer submitted feedback readily available.

Below you’ll find Tuft & Needle Verified Reviews. We send every single customer the same survey 10-14 days after purchase. Raw, real, unfiltered – this feedback is from people just like you.

The best feedback comes from one question.

We know how far a personal recommendation goes.

When reading Tuft & Needle Verified Reviews, you won’t come across any gold stars. Instead, we ask our customers two simple questions that tell us everything we need to know.

On a scale of 0-10, how likely are you to recommend Tuft & Needle to a friend? Why did you pick that number?

There’s no higher compliment than our customers recommending us to their inner circle, and there’s no greater way to strengthen our company than knowing why they wouldn’t.
