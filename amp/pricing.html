NHI Medical Procedures and Fees

We understand that you can compare prices and that cost is a factor. However, we often see patients that consult and schedule corrective surgery with us because they chose a less qualified doctor for their initial surgery.

If you want to be confident that your surgery will be done right the first time, meet with an NHI Medical doctor who can artistically transform your look.

There are no salespeople or “special consultants”. All consultations are with an NHI Medical doctor, and no commitment is required to schedule a consultation.

The Cost of Hair Transplant Procedures

At NHI Medical, our fee is based on the number of grafts placed during your procedure. Each patient is unique, so we recommend speaking with one of our surgeons to get a better understanding of your procedure and pricing.

Scalp MicroPigmentation

  • $3500 for one section
  • $5500 for two sections
  • $7500 for three sections
  • $9000 for entire head

Pricing includes THREE (3) SMP sessions based on the doctor's discretion. (A minimum of three is always required, but in some cases the doctor will advise an additional session to ensure the pigment color reaches the full richness of color. Please be advised the doctor may feel the results will look better with four sessions in which case the patient will not be charged.

All three sessions included in the SMP fees must be completed within one year from the start date of the first SMP session or additional touch up fees will apply. It is recommended that the sessions be done within two to three weeks of each other over a two-three month period.

No refunds will be issued if three sessions are not utilized, as we do not recommend less for the best results.

Deposits and Fees

A $1000 deposit is required to reserve your procedure date.

This deposit is refundable if the request is made at least 10 days prior to your scheduled procedure date. Your balance is due a minimum of 7 business days prior to your procedure date. There is a $3500 minimum fee per procedure. If a patient is not able to receive the planned number of grafts, only the grafts actually received will be charged (over the $3500 minimum).
