Welcome to The GentleManual

We’re not interested in resurrecting a century-old ideal of what it means to be a “gentleman”. No, the GentleManual is reclaiming the term and redefining it for the modern man.

While other men’s blogs fixate on the trends of the moment, we focus on dependable, time-tested advice that will serve a man well for a lifetime. If you’re looking to put your best foot forward, we’ve got every men’s style guide, sartorial advice and fashion tip you could possibly need.

So what does it mean to be a modern gentleman? An insatiable curiosity for travel, life, men’s style, the culinary arts, and self-improvement. Whether you’re from Los Angeles, New York or across the globe, we’ve got the best advice for men out there.

The GentleManual exists to help guide you along your journey. That’s why we work tirelessly to make your best self a reality.

Cheers, and good luck out there.
