Patient Care and Safety

At NHI Medical, patient safety and care has always been our greatest concern. We have always upheld the highest standards in sterilization and cleanliness using the follow protocols:

  • Only one patient per procedure room per day.

  • Doctors and medical technicians wear gowns, gloves and facemasks in the procedure room with our patients.

  • Procedure Rooms and Surgical Tools are thoroughly cleaned and sterilized before each patient’s procedure.

Personal Protective Equipment

We have always and will continue to use PPE for the safety of our patients and staff. Patients also have the option to wear face masks and gloves provided by our office. Hand sanitizer is also available throughout our office as well.

Sterilization

We adhere to the strictest guidelines for cleanliness and safety throughout our entire office.

Thorough cleaning and sterilization of the operating room, tools, equipment, and exterior rooms is completed each day after every procedure.
